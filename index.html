Soundcloud music visualizer: play music from Soundcloud and visualize using various presets.
For more information, visit the project's Github page

Instructions
Paste the URL of a Soundcloud track or playlist into form and press enter.

Example track: https://soundcloud.com/stanwmusic/stan-williams-steve-grisham-pug-baker
Example playlist: https://soundcloud.com/stanwmusic/sets/stan-williams

Controls
space bar - toggle play/pause
right/left arrow - skip forward/backwards through playlist
Preset name
